What Is Fun For Me?

Fun for me is adventure, new experiences, and learning.

Ari

December 1, 2018

In December 2018, I rescued Ari from the animal shelter. She was 1 year old at the time, and she is a Puggle (Pug mixed with a Beagle). She likes to play fetch, tug, and eat everything she can get her paws on.

The Toledo Zoo

August 15, 2017

In August of 2017, I went to the Toledo Zoo with my family. When I was there I fed a giraffe the most expensive lettuce on Earth, ziplined over the zoo, and bungee jumped from a 100 foot tower.

Duolingo Spanish

December 12, 2016

Speaking another language has always been of interest to me, and I worked with the DuoLingo app to accomplish that. I finished all the lessons for Spanish, and I have worked on Italian. Chinese was too difficult. Best of all, the app was free to use.

Martial Arts

March 20, 2015

While I was at Michigan State, I practiced martial arts twice each week. It took me two and a half years to earn a black belt, and it was well worth the time and effort. I recommend martial arts to anyone that wants to have more patience, self-discipline, and confidence.

Dream Center of Pontiac

October 26, 2018

During the fall of 2018, I volunteered to serve lunch at Dream Center of Pontiac. I enjoy spending time helping those in need and it was a humbling experience to work with people in my local community.

Rome, Italy

July 8, 2014

While I was at Michigan State, I studied abroad in Rome, Italy. I traveled to the Vatican, Florence, Naples, Pompeii, Venice, and Ostia. My favorite place was the Vatican and seeing the Pope address the people during his Sunday morning speeches.

Summer Serve Initiative

July 19, 2018

During the Summer of 2018, I participated in the Summer Serve Initiative. For this volunteer experience, I sanded an outdoor porch and painted the basement walls of a house in Pontiac. Shortly after renovating the house, women recovering from abuse moved into the safe haven. It was a pleasure serving the community.

Headspace Meditation

November 7, 2017

Daily meditation reduces stress and improves self-awareness, as a result focusing and patience become easier. I have been doing meditation for two years and counting.

Udemy Tutorials

March 13, 2018

Learning a new skill is one of my favorite activities. In October of 2017 I discovered Udemy, and I spend a lot of my free time learning from the tutors on the website. Some of the skills I learned include sketching and developing websites using Bootstrap and Angular.

College Football

September 17, 2016

Since I graduated from two universities with nationally recognized football programs, people usually ask me who I root for when they play each other. When Michigan State University played University of Notre Dame in September of 2016, I kept it neutral.

Naval Ship Tour

March 7, 2017

In March 2017, I toured a Naval ship and submarine in San Diego, California. During that tour, I got a glimpse of life at sea and in the Navy. From what I could tell, military life is not easy. Thank you to all active duty military and Veterans for your service.

San Francisco, CA

March 10, 2016

For spring break of 2016, the ESTEEM program traveled to San Francisco. We walked the city, drove down Lombard Street, and got the standard picture of the Golden Gate Bridge. We saw Stanford University's campus, and toured companies, such as Facebook, Uber, and Zeltiq.
