What Is Fun For Me?

Fun for me is adventure, new experiences, and learning.

The Toledo Zoo

August 15, 2017

Every once in a while, I go to the zoo to see some cool animals. Back in August of 2017, I went to the Toledo Zoo with my family. I fed giraffes, pet goats, saw tigers, crocodiles, and elephants, ziplined over part of the zoo, and even bungee jumped from a 100 foot tower!

Duolingo Spanish

December 12, 2016

Speaking another language has always been of interest to me. Naturally, I was thrilled to find an app that would help me learn another language for free! I completed the Duolingo Spanish tree, and I have also worked on learning Italian!

Martial Arts

March 20, 2015

Martial arts is a fulfilling experience that cultivates the mind, body, and spirit. It boosts confidence and improves body language. Earning a black belt in martial arts was one of the most satisfying times of my life because the journey to wear it was challenging and full of obstacles.

Rome, Italy

July 8, 2014

While I was at Michigan State, I studied abroad in Rome, Italy. It was an amazing experience that opened my eyes to other cultures. Some of the places I traveled to were Florence, Naples, Pompeii, Venice, Ostia, and the Vatican. Of all those places, my favorite was Rome and the Colosseum.

Udemy Tutorials

November 6, 2017

Learning a new skill is one of my favorite activities. In October of 2017 I stumbled upon Udemy Tutorials, and I spend a lot of my free time learning from the tutors on the website. Some of my lessons are sketching and developing websites using Bootstrap and Angular.

Headspace Meditation

November 7, 2017

Daily meditation helps mitigate stress, improve focus, and heighten patience. I have been doing meditation for two years and counting.

College Football

September 17, 2016

Since I graduated from two universities with top football programs, people usually ask me who I root for when they play head to head. When Michigan State University played University of Notre Dame in September of 2016, I kept it neutral.

Naval Ship Tour

March 7, 2017

In March 2017, I toured a Naval ship and submarine in San Diego, California. It was a ton of fun, and I got a glimpse of life at sea and in the Navy. From what I could tell, military life is hard. Thank you to all those that have served.

San Francisco, CA

March 10, 2016

For spring break of 2016, the ESTEEM program traveled to San Francisco. We traveled the city, drove down Lombard Street, saw the San Francisco bridge, walked around Stanford University's campus, and even toured companies, such as Facebook, Uber, and Zeltiq.
